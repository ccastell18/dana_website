Energy Healing

Reiki is a mind, body and spirit intervention that supports our overall health and acknowledges the person as a “whole”. Reiki is an ancient form of energy based medicine that originates from indigenous Tibetan practice. It’s name derives from Japanese terms Rei meaning universe or spirit and ki meaning energy. Reiki is a hands-on method that promotes healing by channeling the gentle life force energy from the practitioner to the client to rebalance and enhance the body’s inherent healing response. Reiki is considered a complimentary healing intervention that is not intended to take the place of allopathic medicine. It is apart of an integrative approach to healing and optimum health and wellness.

About Dana Marie

Dana Marie Richards, M.A., is a reiki master, channeler, psychic medium, intuitive healer, spiritual coach and shamanic practitioner. She fluidly integrates these holistic modalities as a co-creator in your own healing journey of mind, body and spirit. Through her natural abilities as a medium, Dana Marie can connect with your loved ones and spirit guides bringing you insightful, honest, authentic and loving messages from the spirit world, only delivering to you what you need at that time. Dana Marie's spiritual curriculum began back in 2009, when a shaman healer told her that holistic healing was to be her life's path. Since then, she has earned a Master's degree in Integrative Health Studies and Wellness Coaching, guided imagery certification, a certification as a Usui Reiki Master and discovered her divine skillset as a channeler, psych and spirit medium. She continues to be a dedicated student of women's empowerment based in the sacred knowledge of shamanic tradition and mediumship. Inspired by her love of mind, body and spirit medicine, and her own healing journey, Dana Marie powerfully serves others by creating a sacred and loving healing space to help you discover, shift, release, nourish, heal and lift your spirit to reaching your highest potential.
